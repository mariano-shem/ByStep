EXCUSE LETTER

Procedure

    Note:

  • The student must secure an excuse slip within one week from the time the student reported to school after the absence(s); otherwise, the student will lose the privilege to be excused.

  • The student must secure an admission slip within three days from the time the student reported to school after the absence(s); otherwise, the student will not be allowed to join the class at the next meeting.

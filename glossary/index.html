GLOSSARY

DOCUMENTS

Affidavit of Loss

A sworn statement confirming the physical loss of a property. Contains all the details regarding the loss.

Birth Certificate

A document that records all the vital information regarding a person's birth including the name, time, place, parents, etc.

×

Blue Form (Student's Copy)

Similar to the enrollment form, a blue form is the student's copy of enrollment. Contains information about the student, assessment, and details about the course taken by the student (i.e., subjects, instructors, schedule, room assignment, etc.).

×

Clearance

One of the requirements for students to be able to take the final examination. A ½ sized special paper that contains student's information and requires signatures of various/involved persons and Offices.

×

Enrollment Form

Blue-colored document which the student is required to complete to be able to enroll in the College. Requires student's information and signatures of various/involved Offices; and contains details about the course taken by the student (i.e., subjects, instructors, schedule, room assignment, etc.).

×

Examination Permit

A ¼ sized colored paper that contains the student's name, course & year, permit number, and signature of the person who issued the permit. The student is required to fill out the enrolled subjects so that he/she can take the examination and the proctor can sign on the said examination permit.

Excuse Slip

A document that needs to be secured if the student wishes to be excused from the class. Requires the name of the student, course & year, date/s of absences, subject/s where the student was absent, and the student's signature. It also requires the signature of the Guidance Counselor.

Final Grades Form

A ½ sized special paper that will be containing the grades of the student in corresponding to all the subjects the student enrolled. It requires the student's name, course & year, subjects, and instructors' names.

Good Moral Character

A certificate issued by any Guidance Office to confirm that a former student of an institution has shown model behavior during the time of his/her enrollment.

Honorable Dismissal

A document that certifies the student has no pending liabilities with the school and is qualified for transfer to another educational institution.

×

Library Card

An identification card similar to school ID, a library card is made exclusively at the GCT Library which contains the student's photo, name, course & year, ID number, library card number, and the student's signature.

×

School ID

Made exclusively at the GCT Internet Laboratory which contains the student's photo, name, course & year, ID number, signature, and the person to contact in case of emergency.

SHS Report Card (Form 138)

An official document that records the student's academic performance in Senior High School.

×

Student Information Sheet

Included in the enrollment form. Requires details about the student and his/her parent/guardian as well as the signature of the student.

Transcript of Records

An official document that serves as a complete record of a student's academic achievements during their studies which includes records of courses taken, grades received, number of credits earned, etc.

LOCATION

Assessor's Office

The Assessor's Office is located at the ground floor of Building B (Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building). Students can get an assessment of their payment here as well as obtain a certificate of clearance for their transcript of records.

×

Building A

Building A is also known as Don Florencio M. Garcia Building. The Guidance Office is located in this building.

×

Building B

Building B is also known as Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building. This building contains the Internet Laboratory, the SaaS Office, the SSC Office and the Assessor's Office.

×

Building C

Building C is also known as the Eric R. Garcia Building. The Library is located in this building.

×

Building D

The Administration Building is called Building D. The HAC's Office, Registrar's Office, Treasurer's Office, and EVP Office are all located here.

×

EVP Office

The EVP Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building. Here, the students can get their clearance and enrollment form signed by the Executive Vice President.

×

Guidance Office

The Guidance Office is located on the 2nd floor of Building A (Don Florencio M. Garcia Building). Students can process their excuse letter and can secure their admission slip.

×

HAC's Office

The HAC's Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building. Students can get their enrollment form and clearance signed, if needed.

×

Internet Laboratory

The Internet Laboratory is located on the 3rd floor of Building B (Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building). Freshmen and transferees can obtain their school ID while old students can renew their School ID (for loss of ID) here.

×

Library

The Library is located on the 4th floor of building C ( Eric R. Garcia Building). Students can get their library cards and process them here. Additionally, students can turn in their library cards here for clearance signatures.

×

Registrar's Office

The Registrar's Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building. The Registrar's Office includes seven windows, each with a specific purpose. Students can seek advice from the Registrar's Office for the subject loading. Additionally, students can obtain a sign from the college registrar and the NSTP coordinator for clearance. This is also where you may get your transcript of records.

×

SaaS Office

The SaaS Office is located at the ground floor of Building B (Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building) near the exit gate.

×

SSC Office

The SSC Office is located on the second floor of Building B (Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building). Students can settle their penalties here.

×

Treasurer's Office

The Treasurer's Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building. Students can settle and pay their tuition fees and any necessary fees that are stated in the assessment here. Examination permits can be obtained by the students at the Cashier window next to the Treasurer's Office.

LOCATION

Assessor's Office

The Assessor's Office is located at the ground floor of Building B (Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building). Students can get an assessment of their payment here as well as obtain a certificate of clearance for their transcript of records.

Building A

Building A is also known as Don Florencio M. Garcia Building. The Guidance Office is located in this building.

Building B

Building B is also known as Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building. This building contains the Internet Laboratory, the SaaS Office, the SSC Office and the Assessor's Office.

Building C

Building C is also known as the Eric R. Garcia Building. The Library is located in this building.

Building D

The Administration Building is called Building D. The HAC's Office, Registrar's Office, Treasurer's Office, and EVP Office are all located here.

EVP Office

The EVP Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building. Here, the students can get their clearance and enrollment form signed by the Executive Vice President.

Guidance Office

The Guidance Office is located on the 2nd floor of Building A (Don Florencio M. Garcia Building). Students can process their excuse letter and can secure their admission slip.

HAC's Office

The HAC's Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building. Students can get their enrollment form and clearance signed, if needed.

Internet Laboratory

The Internet Laboratory is located on the 3rd floor of Building B (Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building). Freshmen and transferees can obtain their school ID while old students can renew their School ID (for loss of ID) here.

Library

The Library is located on the 4th floor of building C ( Eric R. Garcia Building). Students can get their library cards and process them here. Additionally, students can turn in their library cards here for clearance signatures.

Registrar's Office

The Registrar's Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building. The Registrar's Office includes seven windows, each with a specific purpose. Students can seek advice from the Registrar's Office for the subject loading. Additionally, students can obtain a sign from the college registrar and the NSTP coordinator for clearance. This is also where you may get your transcript of records.

SaaS Office

The SaaS Office is located at the ground floor of Building B (Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building) near the exit gate.

SSC Office

The SSC Office is located on the second floor of Building B (Doña Enrica R. Garcia Building). Students can settle their penalties here.

Treasurer's Office

The Treasurer's Office is located at the ground floor of the Administration Building. Students can settle and pay their tuition fees and any necessary fees that are stated in the assessment here. Examination permits can be obtained by the students at the Cashier window next to the Treasurer's Office.
